Key Strategy Notes

The Shift in 2025

EF moved from "open application" to "proactive, needs-driven". Now targeting ecosystem-wide impact and real apps with users, not just protocol work.

L2 vs EF Approach

Base/Arbitrum/Optimism: Faster turnaround, care about users/traction.
Ethereum Foundation: Still leans technical/public-goods, but "Usability" wishlist + Hackternships are best for pure app ideas.
