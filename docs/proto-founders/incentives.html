Replicate & Simplify on Other Blockchains

All 30 examples are already live and documented. You can recreate many of them in simpler formats — using fewer features, lighter smart contracts, or more accessible chains like Solana, Base, Polygon, or even TON for Telegram mini-apps. Lower gas fees and faster iteration make these ideas perfect for your Project Engine.

Why Replicate These?

  • 1
    Simpler Versions Work Better
    Many full-featured games can be distilled into core mechanics (e.g., basic breeding or simple quests) that run efficiently on cheaper or faster chains.
  • 2
    Cross-Chain Experimentation
    Port mechanics from Ethereum/Ronin to Solana or Aptos for lower costs and higher throughput while keeping on-chain data capture secure.
  • 3
    Focus on Data Capture
    Use immutable smart contracts for progress, rewards, and reputation — even in minimal viable versions — to ensure tamper-proof, verifiable results.
