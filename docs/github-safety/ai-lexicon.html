AI Lexicon for X Profiles

A curated glossary of hashtags and labels for disclosing AI uses on X (formerly Twitter). Inspired by emerging trends in 2025, this lexicon promotes transparency in an era of bots, assistants, and creators.

Explore the Lexicon

Search or click on terms below for Wiki-style explainers. These labels can be added to bios or posts for clarity—e.g., "#AIAugmented Human". Drawn from reported AI instances on X, including sales agents, scam bots, and creative tools.

Collaborative & Augmented AI

#AIAugmented

Definition: Describes profiles where AI enhances human capabilities, such as content ideation or research, without replacing the user. Common in creators and professionals using tools like Grok or ChatGPT for brainstorming.

Example Use: "AI-Augmented Writer | #AIAugmented"

Context: Emerged in 2025 discussions on X about ethical AI collaboration, contrasting with full automation.

#AICollaborator

Definition: For accounts treating AI as a creative partner, e.g., co-authoring posts or generating visuals. Highlights symbiotic human-AI workflows.

Example Use: "Digital Artist x AI | #AICollaborator"

Context: Popular among indie developers and artists on X, as seen in threads about AI tools like Midjourney.

Commercial & Sales AI

#AILeadGen

Definition: Labels profiles using AI for lead generation, such as scraping LinkedIn or qualifying prospects via bots.

Example Use: "B2B Growth Hacker | #AILeadGen"

Context: Widespread in 2025 sales automation posts on X, with templates for AI-driven outreach.

#AISalesBot

Definition: Automated accounts for sales interactions, like voice agents calling leads or drafting emails.

Example Use: "AI Sales Closer | #AISalesBot"

Context: Featured in X promotions for tools like AI Voice Sales Agents, emphasizing efficiency in cold outreach.

#AIVoiceAgent

Definition: Profiles powered by voice-based AI for customer service or prospecting, e.g., handling inbound calls.

Example Use: "24/7 Support Bot | #AIVoiceAgent"

Context: Gained traction in 2025 with templates for dental, e-commerce, and travel industries on X.

Creative & Generative AI

#AICreator

Definition: For users generating content like images, text, or music with AI tools, disclosing the tech behind their work.

Example Use: "AI-Powered Storyteller | #AICreator"

Context: Common in X communities sharing AI art and writing, promoting transparency amid deepfake concerns.

#AIArtist

Definition: Profiles focused on AI-generated visuals, such as photo replacements or interior designs.

Example Use: "Generative Art | #AIArtist"

Context: Inspired by 2025 X posts on tools like PhotoAI and InteriorAI, disrupting traditional creative roles.

Utility & Automation AI

#AIAutomator

Definition: Accounts automating repetitive tasks, like research or scheduling, to save time.

Example Use: "Workflow Optimizer | #AIAutomator"

Context: Highlighted in X threads on reclaiming hours via AI agents for non-technical users.

#AIAssistant

Definition: General-purpose helpers for queries, reminders, or data analysis, akin to virtual aides.

Example Use: "Your AI Sidekick | #AIAssistant"

Context: Evolved from 2025 bot-tagging on X, distinguishing helpful from spammy automation.

Risky & Malicious AI

#ScamBot

Definition: Fraudulent AI accounts mimicking humans for scams, like promo DMs or phishing.

Example Use: (Avoid; for flagging) "Flagged Scam | #ScamBot"

Context: X's 2025 "Automated" tags spotlight these, with engines flagging crypto shills.

#DeepfakeScammer

Definition: Profiles using deepfakes for impersonation or fraud, e.g., fake video calls.

Example Use: (Warning label) "Deepfake Alert | #DeepfakeScammer"

Context: Rising in late 2025 X warnings about AI fraud trends on platforms like TikTok.
