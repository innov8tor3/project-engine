The Human Cost

Administrative burdens create learning, compliance, and psychological costs — affecting millions daily.

🧠

Mental Health Impact

High frustration, anxiety, and stress from paperwork. Contributes to burnout and reduced autonomy, especially for benefits and healthcare tasks.

Psychological costs are particularly severe for low-income and mentally ill individuals. Studies show direct links to poorer well-being and health outcomes.
⏱️

Time & Productivity Loss

UK office workers spend ~31% of time on admin. US physicians lose nearly 9 hours/week. This drains focus from meaningful work.

Adults may lose over 5 years of life to admin tasks. Entrepreneurs and professionals report major impacts on career satisfaction.
💸

Financial Hardship

Unclaimed benefits, delayed care, medical debt, and rent arrears. Vulnerable groups are hit hardest.

Administrative burdens lead to forgone aid worth billions annually and push people into debt cycles.

Dive Deeper

📊
Prevalence of Admin Aversion
Data entry, filing, and email management consistently rank among the most hated tasks.
  • 55% of workers would consider leaving jobs with excessive admin load
  • Government inefficiency often cited as evidence of systemic difficulty
  • Disproportionately affects low-income, elderly, and those with poor health
