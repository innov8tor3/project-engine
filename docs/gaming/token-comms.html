2-Week Token-Oriented Community Comms Strategy

This lean schedule focuses on high-impact posts every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday across channels like X, Telegram, and Discord. It builds momentum in Week 1 and sustains engagement in Week 2. Each post is multifaceted to maximize interaction. Expect consolidated content: updates, polls, teasers, and events timed for peak hours. Track metrics like replies and reposts to refine. Newsletters on Fridays recap the week and gather feedback.

Week 1: Build Momentum and Excitement

Day 1 (Monday) - Project Update & Educational Thread

Combined post: "Q1 roadmap progress: Staking launches soon—plus a quick explainer on token rewards!" Include a poll for feature feedback and a small giveaway teaser.

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

Post early morning (e.g., 9 AM UTC) to kick off the week. This builds trust with transparent progress and educates members on value, sparking discussions and shares. Expect polls to run all day for ongoing engagement.

Day 3 (Wednesday) - Fun & Interactive Post

Fun content: A meme tying into market trends with your token, plus user spotlight: "Share your success stories in replies!" Host a quick Q&A in chat post-publish.

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

Mid-week boost around noon (e.g., 12 PM UTC). Lightens the mood, encourages replies, and humanizes the community. Q&A follows immediately for real-time interaction, keeping energy high mid-week.

Day 5 (Friday) - Event-Focused Announcement

Announcement: "Join our AMA next Monday—tease: Airdrop incentives for attendees!" End with newsletter recapping the week and a feedback survey.

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

Late afternoon (e.g., 4 PM UTC) to build weekend anticipation. Creates FOMO for the upcoming event. Newsletter emails go out evening, summarizing highlights and collecting insights for Week 2 adjustments.

Week 2: Sustain and Gather Insights

Day 8 (Monday) - Transparent Update with AMA

Update: "Volatility check-in and dev adaptations—live AMA now!" Incorporate polls on concerns and reward engagers (e.g., Discord badges).

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

Start the week strong at 10 AM UTC with the live AMA. Addresses issues transparently, turning potential negativity into dialogue. Polls and rewards sustain discussions throughout the day.

Day 10 (Wednesday) - Incentive & Community-Driven Content

Post: "Giveaway for friend referrals + share your token use-case ideas—we'll feature winners!" Add an educational infographic on integrations.

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

Mid-week at 1 PM UTC. Drives growth through incentives and user ideas, fostering co-creation. Educational element adds value, with replies encouraged for ongoing activity.

Day 12 (Friday) - Wrap-Up with Partnership Tease

Tease: "Upcoming collab hints + Week 2 highlights." Include newsletter with survey results and a call-to-action for roadmap input. Optional casual voice chat.

**Summary:** What to Expect & When

End the week at 3 PM UTC. Recaps progress, teases future excitement, and gathers final feedback via newsletter (sent evening). Voice chat if needed for deeper discussions, wrapping on a high note.
