Oasis Licensing via Smart Contract

Deploy a Solana-based revenue-share smart contract (using Anchor/Metaplex) to formalize licensing. Communities mint an "Oasis License NFT" granting unlimited access to Oasis tools, in exchange for:

  • Advertising Rights: Banner/promoted posts in community channels (Discord, X, events) highlighting SC Bonanza as an enabler.
  • Fee Slice: 5-10% of fees from community-referred SC creations/mints (tracked via affiliate wallet).
  • Self-Service Perks: Built-in audits, drag-and-drop UI, NFT integration for video-linked contracts—slashing costs by 99% vs. agencies.
SC Mechanics:
  • License Deployment: Community lead mints NFT (~0.01 SOL); unlocks Oasis API keys.
  • Rev Share: On-chain escrow releases fees weekly; transparent via Solana Explorer.
  • Termination: 30-day notice clause for flexibility.
Implementation: Prototype via Helius RPC; audit for top vulnerabilities. Total setup: 1-2 weeks.
