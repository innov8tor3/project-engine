Quest #9 - QuestPreneur

Turning Ideas into Entrepreneurial Ventures on Solana

Quest Overview

This quest is Phase 1 for Project Engine, asking to engage game players in pursuit of enterprise, and provide players with an introductory landscape document.

This landscape aims to provide a hot topic tag into a quest, and draw a likely founder further into the realms of making it to being an entrepreneur. The aim is to create revenue and employment options in the UK, or elsewhere that wants to play.

ANIMATING A QUEST FOR GAMERS

How can this work? First a quest definition is needed. Project Engine Phase 1 - QUEST1Q already has ready enough capability to do this, we can see from the emerging pages in the Project Engine Hot Topic Pages.

So the first question is how to animate and ignite this first Phase. We can do this by providing the quest outline to a friendly gaming community - Moonboi could be that community - and setting a competition for entrants to create a simple landscape document, with a problem to solve, the proposed solution, and what obstacles are likely along that path. The solution will be judged by AI as to the potential real world impact, the value that could be generated - a sliding score between fledgling and excellent project management - and the realism used in terms of the obstacles to be overcome and the mitigating activity needed to smooth progress past those obstacles.

WINNERS

Winners would get a tailored Quest page built for them, and digital onchain project assets to help them move forward in discussion with funders and other interested parties. This can include smart contract specs, pitch decks and so on.

Winners would get supported access to the Solana community, and the Solana growth ecosystem, via a variety of means. It would also mean graduation, entry to phase 2 of Project Engine, namely how to build a platform that would support the Quest outlined in phase 1. This would seek to accelerate tech learning through Vibe Coding, but also access to experienced Solana engineers who can help with the complicated aspects of any tech build and the nuance about the Solana Landscape and ecosystems, such as which DEX platform Solana recommends and so on.

SUSTAINABILITY

We will need to develop a sustainability model. For example, say entrance is free, but a smart contract is signed to share 20% of any future funding obtained, with an agreed sunset clause. This could be developed in discussion with Moonboi, as to ways this could work.

NEXT STEPS

The project-engine founder now needs to follow a quest regarding a range of different options to see which will yield fruit.
